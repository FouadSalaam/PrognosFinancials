About Our Methodology

The PrognosFinancials Score is an advanced financial analysis tool designed to provide a clear, concise, and actionable assessment of a company's financial health and stability. By analyzing key financial metrics, the PrognosFinancials Score helps you quickly understand a company's position and identify potential risks or opportunities.

Interpreting Your Score

1. Green is the Safe Zone

This indicates that the company is in **good financial health** with a low risk of financial distress or bankruptcy. The business is likely to be stable and sustainable.

2. Yellow is the Grey Zone

This suggests the company's financial health is **ambiguous**. Caution is advised, and further analysis is recommended to determine its true position.

3. Red is the Distress Zone

This indicates that the company is at a **high risk of financial distress or bankruptcy** within the next two years. Immediate action and a comprehensive review of its financial statements are highly recommended.
